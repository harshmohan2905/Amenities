Amenities


Everything that makes staying at Radisson Blu an experience you’ll never forget


* Custom designed rooms

Every single one of our rooms is special so that all of our guests have a boutique hotel experience. All rooms boast luxurious bathrooms, hand picked vintage furniture bought in Amien, and handmade beds for unbelievable comfort.


* Room service

Whether you want breakfast in bed, or crave a nightcap–we’ve got you covered.


* Connecting rooms

Our rooms are highly adaptable. We love making space for guests. The more the merrier. Ask us about our combined room possibilities.


* Handmade dreams

Nothing is more relaxing than a great night’s sleep. We offer each of our guests a custom, handmade bed created exclusively for Vesper Hotel by Dutch furnituremakers Softline.


* High speed internet

Who likes slow wi-fi? We’ve made sure all our rooms come with a fast connection, so you can slow down.


* Car parking

Parking is available at the boulevard in front of the hotel. You can obtain a parking permit at the reception for Rs.500,- per room per night.


* Complementary breakfast

Radisson Blu Hotel offers all our guests a full breakfast every morning in our beautiful lobby. Our chef is always available to cater to specific requests.


* Laundry & dry cleaning

We offer 24-hour laundry & dry cleaning services.


* Our lobby

We consider our lobby the heart of our hotel. It’s where we celebrate a day well spent and get ready for the next one. We serve exquisite cocktails and fine finger foods there, as well as beautiful wines right from our wine cellar.


* Byredo

All our rooms are stocked with Byredo cosmetics.
